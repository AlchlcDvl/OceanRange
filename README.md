# The Ocean Range
Ocean Range is a Slime Rancher mod in active development that dives into a whole new region of the Far, Far Range‚Äîan expansive, oceanic world brought to life through community creativity and collaboration. Featuring brand-new slimes, foods, and gordo encounters, Ocean Range also introduces a story-driven experience that expands the Slime Rancher universe in a fresh, narrative-focused direction. Funded and inspired by the community, this project is envisioned as a continuation of the various ranch expansions like Mochi's, Ogden's and Victor's, celebrating the imaginative spirit that defines Slime Rancher at its core.

Make sure to read the known issues before reporting bugs! Duplicates will be removed.

## Slimes
- Rosi Slimes: The aquatic cousins of the pink slimes, you can pink these slimes anywhere!
- Coco Slimes: The hard-bottomed coconut slimes that can hurt pretty bad upon contact! Watch your heads!
- Mine Slimes: The cousins of the boom slime, don't touch them or else they go kaboom!
- Lantern Slimes: Another cousin of the phosphor slimes, lantern slimes sleep during the day and run away if you wake them up!
- Sand Slimes: The land dwelling cousin of the puddle slimes, the sand slimes are low maintenance!
- Mesmer Slimes: Hypnotic and awe inducing slimes that are actually playful pacifists!
- Hermit Slimes: Reclusive and shy slimes that like to nibble on fruits in their shells!
- Goldfish Slimes: The rare, timid and beautiful slimes adapted to the sea!
